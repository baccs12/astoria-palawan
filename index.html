The Astoria Palawan

The Huma Island Resort & Spa is a nestled on a remote island in Busuanga- Palawan and set as a 5* Deluxe resort offering breath taking views, gracious living, untouched underwater marine life surrounded by a dozen of sunken Japanese warships and planes which is hailed as one of the top 10 best diving sites in the world.

The Huma Island Resort & Spa is a multi-million USD investment comprises of total 81 villas each with individual Jacuzzi, 7 dining outlets, fitness centre, library, kids room and SPA centre with glass bottom flooring for the first time presented in Philippines.
